Latent Semantic Analysis in Greek Parliament Documents

After the preprocessing of the data-documents, there were left ~700,000 documents.

So we read the inverted index to get all the terms and the documents
We found ~440,000 terms, and we used COO format to build the term-to-doc matrix

We used the TruncatedSVD function of the sklearn library to run SVD.
We reduced the ~440,000 of the data 300, 400, 500 and 800 dimensions. We tried to use the total 'energy' of the concept and keep those that retain the 90% of the information. However, this was practically impossible as it took 5 hours to make
So we run the algorithm for:
(1) 300 dimensions: it takes approximately 5-8 minutes and retains the ~50% of the information
(2) 400 dimensions: it takes approximately ~12 minutes and retains the ~53% of the information
(3) 500 dimensions: it takes approximately 15 - 20 minutes and retains the ~55% of the information
(4) 600 dimensions: it takes approximately ~25 minutes and retains the ~60% of the information
(5) 800 dimensions: it takes approximately ~30 minutes and retains the ~66% of the information
