Biggest Speeches by Government by Political Party

This page is dedicated to the results of the question:
"How many words are said per government per political party"

The results are normalized with the number of members per party.

With blue color it is stated the governing party or parties
and with red color the party with the most words on average.
Interestingly the party with the most words is often identified
as the opposition party and most of the times is on the left wing.
